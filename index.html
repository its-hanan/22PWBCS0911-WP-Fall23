About Us

Café De Peshawar is your favorite neighborhood café, serving a variety of delightful dishes and beverages. We take pride in offering a cozy and inviting atmosphere for our customers. Welcome to Cafe de Peshawar, where rich traditions meet modern delights in a warm and inviting atmosphere. Established with a passion for exquisite flavors and heartfelt hospitality, Cafe de Peshawar is more than just a café; it's a culinary journey that celebrates the vibrant culture and authentic tastes of Peshawar, Pakistan. At Cafe de Peshawar, we take pride in crafting an experience that goes beyond the ordinary. Our menu is a testament to the culinary heritage of Peshawar, featuring a delightful array of traditional dishes prepared with love and care. From aromatic chai and freshly baked naans to mouthwatering kebabs and fragrant biryanis, every bite is a homage to the rich gastronomic heritage of the region. But Cafe de Peshawar is not just about food; it's about creating a welcoming space where friends and families gather, conversations flow, and memories are made. Our cozy ambiance, inspired by the beauty of Peshawar, provides the perfect backdrop for intimate gatherings, business meetings, or simply unwinding with a cup of our signature tea. At the heart of our establishment is a team of dedicated individuals who are passionate about serving you the finest flavors of Peshawar. With a commitment to quality and authenticity, we source the freshest ingredients, blend traditional recipes with a contemporary twist, and present dishes that tantalize your taste buds and warm your soul. We invite you to embark on a culinary adventure with us at Cafe de Peshawar. Whether you are a connoisseur of Pakistani cuisine or a curious explorer seeking new flavors, our cafe promises an unforgettable experience. Join us and savor the essence of Peshawar, one plate at a time. "Discover the enchanting flavors of Peshawar at Cafe de Peshawar, where every dish tells a story and every bite is a journey into the heart of Pakistani cuisine. Indulge in the exquisite taste of tradition, crafted with passion and served with warmth. Join us and experience the magic of Peshawari hospitality, where every visit is not just a meal, but a celebration of flavors, culture, and cherished moments. Come, let your taste buds travel!"
